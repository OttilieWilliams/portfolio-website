About me

I'm exploring the world of tech as a Trainee on the BT furtHER Digital Intensive Programme (November 2018 - February 2019). Welcome to my portfolio website.

How I got into Tech

I first became curious when my friend Ella (cyberwomen.co.uk) told me about her job as a User Researcher. She was so passionate I wanted to know what all the fuss was about! I taught myself the basics of HTML and Ruby on codecademy.com, attended coding workshops at Bletchley Park, and researched courses, internships and other paths into the industry. I was really excited to be offered a place on the BT furtHER programme, and I'm now two months in.

What I have learnt so far

I've learned some HTML, CSS, Javascript, UX & UI, and Python. I've also spent time on Github and practiced pair programming. There is so much more to learn and I love spending my spare time delving into tech books, getting past coding challenges, and attending coding events - my favourite so far was CodeBar held at Google HQ!
